Returns Management and Reversed Logistics

Returns are an essential part of any firm selling business strategies. It is impossible to avoid them thoroughly, however, they can be kept at a minimum range by safeguarding that all processes are professionally managed and brought to a maturity level. Our Fulfilment Advisor can advise you on how to reduce the amount of returns your business has to deal with. It also helps you manage the entire returns process. We can manage complaints and take care of all the affiliated activities – Starting from receiving returns through product reprocessing or repairing to repacking. In other words: while you quintessence on your core business, so sit back and relax as we handle the rest.

  • Blueprinting and implementation of returns management notions
  • Processing of small and large-volume items
  • Automatic capture of data, including the reason for the return
  • Inspection, validation and refurbishing of incoming returns
  • Assessments (e.g. of textiles, electrical appliances) according to individually determined criteria
  • Repacking in the original packaging
